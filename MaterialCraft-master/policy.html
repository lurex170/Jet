Privacy Policy
What personal information do we collect?

When registering an account on "Your Site", you will be required to enter your Email Address, along with a username and password. Your information is securely stored in our database, and is not shared to any third parties. Additionally, your IP address will be logged whilst browsing the website and will be used to lookup on a spam database to determine if you have previously spammed another forum. Only Administrators can view user IP addresses - your IP will not be shared with any third parties.

Other information such as page referals, private messages, and emails from the forum may be logged for security purposes and are only viewable by the site administrator. All information is only used to prevent misuse of this forum. By using our forums, you agree to this information to be collected.

We won’t share your information with any other organisations for marketing, market research or commercial purposes, and we don’t pass on your details to other websites. Please note that we may share your personal information if we have a legal obligation to do so.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies in order to allow you to use it. Without cookies, the website wouldn't be able to function as we require them in order to keep you logged into your account. You can read more about cookies here: http://www.whatarecookies.com/.

Website usage

By using our website "yoursite.net", you agree to follow all rules explained on the rules forum post. Please note that you must be at least 13 years of age to register for an account. "Your Site" follows all US laws, therefore we will remove any content which violates US laws.

Data Breaches

In the case of a data breach, e.g. user database leaked, which may include email addresses and passwords, you will be notified via email about this. All passwords will be reset, with an email sent to you with instructions on how to recover your account. Please keep in mind we take security seriously.

Contact Us If you wish to contact us for further information, you can either make a post on the forums, or send an email to "youremail@yoursite.net".
